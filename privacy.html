Privacy Policy

At Snazzy Paws Grooming, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information (name, email, phone) via booking and contact forms.
  • Usage data (pages visited, session duration) via analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

  • To confirm and manage appointments.
  • To improve our website and services based on usage patterns.

Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data by emailing privacy@snazzypaws.nz.
